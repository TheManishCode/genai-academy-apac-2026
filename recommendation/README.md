# Decision Intelligence Engine

EcoMind never stops at prediction. The decision layer turns risk into action:

- Flood risk -> pumps, shelter activation, road closure, hospital prep
- AQI risk -> health advisories, school guidance, outdoor shift reduction
- Heat risk -> cooling centers, power monitoring, vulnerable resident outreach
- Hospital load -> staff surge, ambulance diversion, oxygen and bed planning

Each recommendation should include expected impact, owner, urgency, dependencies and confidence.
